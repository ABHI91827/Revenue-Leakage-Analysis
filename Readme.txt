# 📊 E-commerce Revenue Leakage Analysis

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce business facing declining revenue despite stable user traffic.
The goal is to identify conversion drop-offs in the customer journey and uncover key drivers of revenue leakage.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Despite consistent user traffic, the business observed a decline in revenue.
This indicates inefficiencies in the conversion funnel where potential customers drop off before completing purchases.

---

## 🧠 Objective

* Identify drop-off points in the conversion funnel (View → Cart → Purchase)
* Analyze customer behavior (New vs Repeat)
* Evaluate revenue contribution by customer segments
* Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **SQL** – Data querying and funnel analysis
* **Python (Pandas, NumPy)** – Data generation and exploratory analysis
* **Power BI** – Interactive dashboard and visualization

---

## 📁 Dataset

A synthetic dataset was created to simulate real-world e-commerce behavior, including:

* **Events Table** – User interactions (view, cart, purchase)
* **Orders Table** – Transaction-level revenue data
* **Customers Table** – Customer demographics and regions

> Note: The dataset was generated to mimic real business scenarios where actual company data is not publicly available.

---

## 📊 Key Analysis

### 🔹 Conversion Funnel

* View → Purchase Conversion: **83.41%**
* Cart → Purchase Conversion: **87.56%**

### 🔹 Customer Insights

* **66% of customers are repeat buyers**
* Indicates strong customer retention

### 🔹 Revenue Insights

* Repeat customers contribute **~5x more revenue** than new customers
* Revenue is heavily dependent on returning users

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

* ~12% drop-off observed at the checkout stage, indicating revenue leakage
* High repeat customer ratio suggests strong product-market fit
* Business growth is driven more by retention than acquisition

---

## 📈 Dashboard

The Power BI dashboard provides:

* KPI tracking (Revenue, Orders, Customers, Conversion Rates)
* Funnel visualization to identify drop-offs
* Customer segmentation (Repeat vs New)
* Revenue contribution analysis
* Time-based revenue trends

---

## 🧩 Recommendations

* Optimize checkout experience to reduce drop-offs
* Implement cart abandonment strategies (discounts, reminders)
* Focus on retention through loyalty programs and personalization
* Target high-value repeat customers for sustained growth

## 📌 Conclusion

The analysis highlights that while customer engagement is strong, revenue leakage occurs primarily during the checkout stage.
Improving conversion and leveraging repeat customers can significantly enhance business performance.
